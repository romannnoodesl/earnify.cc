What Is Browser Mining?

Browser mining uses Web Workers and WebAssembly to perform proof-of-work hashing directly in a visitor's browser. Unlike traditional advertising, which relies on tracking pixels and third-party cookies, compute-based monetization creates a direct link between user engagement and revenue — no intermediaries, no data collection.

Earnify connects browsers to real mining pools via the Stratum protocol over WebSocket. Every valid share submitted earns cryptocurrency proportional to the computational work performed. With a 1% platform fee and zero data footprint, it offers a privacy-first alternative to ad-supported business models.

The demo above uses the MinotaurX algorithm (ASIC-resistant, CPU-friendly) and connects to the zpool mining pool with auto-exchange to Ravencoin (RVN). It allocates 50% of your CPU threads for a realistic but conservative demonstration.
