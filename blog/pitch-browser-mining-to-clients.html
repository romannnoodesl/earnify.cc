How to Pitch Browser Mining to Your Clients: A Sales Framework

Learn a proven sales framework to pitch browser-based crypto mining to website owners. Overcome objections, project revenue, and close deals with Earnify.

Most website owners you pitch to won’t immediately understand browser mining. They’ll hear “crypto” and think of scams, malware, or something that’ll slow their site to a crawl. Your job as a freelancer or agency is to reframe the conversation: browser mining isn’t a replacement for ads—it’s a privacy-respecting, zero-real-estate revenue layer that works alongside everything else they’re already doing.

This sales framework is built from real conversations with publishers who’ve adopted Earnify. You’ll learn how to surface pain points, present the technology without jargon, handle the three objections that kill deals, and close with a no-risk trial that makes the decision easy.

Understand Your Client’s Monetization Pain Points

Before you mention mining, you need to understand what’s already broken. Most publishers—especially small to mid-sized sites—are stuck in a cycle of diminishing returns from display ads. The numbers tell the story:

  • Average display CPMs have dropped from $2.80 in 2018 to around $1.50 today for non-premium inventory.
  • Ad-blocker usage sits at 42.7% globally (Statista, 2024), meaning nearly half of visitors generate zero ad revenue.
  • GDPR and cookie consent banners reduce measurable ad revenue by another 15–30% because opted-out users can’t be targeted.

Add to that the user experience cost: intrusive interstitials, auto-play video ads, and page bloat that hurts Core Web Vitals. Publishers are desperate for revenue that doesn’t punish their audience. That’s your opening.

Key Insight: The most receptive clients are those who already earn $500–$5,000/month from ads but see ad revenue plateauing or declining. They have traffic but are leaving money on the table—especially from the 40%+ of visitors using ad blockers.

Position Browser Mining as the Solution

Browser mining uses a tiny fraction of a visitor’s CPU to solve cryptographic puzzles while they’re on the site. The site earns cryptocurrency (typically Ravencoin), which is automatically converted to the publisher’s wallet. There are no ads to display, no personal data collected, and no screen real estate consumed. Here’s how to frame it.

The Privacy-First Advantage

In a post-GDPR world, privacy isn’t just a legal requirement—it’s a competitive differentiator. Earnify’s architecture was built for this reality: zero cookies, zero tracking, zero personal data collection. The mining script runs entirely in the browser via Web Workers and WebAssembly, and communicates with mining pools over WebSocket Stratum—no user identifiers are ever transmitted. This means:

  • No cookie consent banner needed for the mining component.
  • Fully compliant with GDPR, CCPA, and ePrivacy directives out of the box.
  • Works even when users reject all tracking—because there is no tracking.

For publishers burned by consent fatigue, this is a powerful selling point. You can say: “This is the only revenue stream that actually increases when users opt out of tracking.”
